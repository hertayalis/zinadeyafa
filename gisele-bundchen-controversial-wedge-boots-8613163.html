See Gisele Bndchen's Controversial Wedge Boots

Publish date: 2024-05-23

Gisele Bündchen may be the queen of rich mom style, but she just reached peak cool mom status with her latest look — and it all came down to her choice in footwear. 

On Thursday, the supermodel was spotted departing her New York City hotel en route for an appearance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon in an outfit that was the sartorial opposite of her classic camel coats and head-to-toe winter whites. Wearing all-black, Bündchen slipped on a double-breasted blazer dress with two rows of buttons down the front and a mini hemline that flared out to the sides. She teamed her dress with sheer black stockings, a matching midi-length wool coat, and a very polarizing boot silhouette. 

On her feet were a pair of black leather knee-highs with rounded toes a controversial wedge heel, taking her outfit from a basic mom's night out to mom gone wild territory. She finished accessorizing with chunky gold hoops and styled her hair in effortless waves swept over her shoulder. A nude lip, glowing skin, and minimal eye makeup completed her glam.

In case you didn't know, Bündchen is currently in the Big Apple promoting her new cookbook Nourish, and earlier that same day, she stopped by The View in another outfit that gave her rich mom aesthetic a fresh update. Layering a camel coat over a luxe knit turtleneck in a charcoal gray shade, the model opted for a surprising combination on bottom, pairing a light-wash denim midi skirt with black pointed-toe stiletto boots.
