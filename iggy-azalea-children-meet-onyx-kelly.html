Publish date: 2024-07-17

Iggy Azalea Children: Meet Onyx Kelly: Iggy Azalea, officially known as Amethyst Amelia Kelly is an Australian rapper, songwriter, and model.

She developed a love for music at a very younger age and has been consistent throughout her career. Azalea moved from Australia to the United States at the age of 16 in order to pursue a career in music.

Iggy Azalea earned public recognition after releasing the music videos for her songs ‘Pussy’ and ‘Two Times’ on YouTube, both of which gained rapid popularity.

Shortly after the release of her debut mixtape, Ignorant Art, she subsequently signed a recording contract with Grand Hustle label, a label owned by American rapper T.I.

Her debut studio album, The New Classic peaked among the top five on several charts worldwide and eventually topped the Billboard Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums.

Azalea became the first non-American female rapper to top the Billboard Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums.

Following the success of her debut album, she went ahead to release a couple of singles before becoming an independent artist and creating her own label, Bad Dreams, through a distribution deal with Empire.

Azalea is a recipient of two American Music Awards, three Billboard Music Awards, an MTV Video Music Award, a People’s Choice Award, four Teen Choice Awards, and four Grammy Award nominations.

In February 2023, Iggy Azalea made headlines after she made an appearance on Emily Ratajkowski’s podcast on Monday, February 20, 2023.

In Monday’s episode of Emily Ratajkowski’s podcast, Iggy Azalea spoke about why she joined OnlyFans back in January almost two years after saying she would never sign up for the platform.

“I’ve made a lot of people so much money off my body, and I got the smallest cut off my own fucking body,” the rapper said on the episode. “And my own work, and my own ideas.”

Iggy Azalea added that she doesn’t feel she should have to apologize for making money off her “own body” and ideas on her OnlyFans account.
