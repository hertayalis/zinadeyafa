See All the Best Celebrity Costumes for Halloween 2021

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Halloween has been extra festive this year, as Spooky Season fans get into the holiday spirit after last year's at-home festivities.

With pandemic-safe parties returning, celebs have gone all out, taking over our Instagram feeds with intricate and jaw-dropping costumes.

Whether it's Hailey Bieber posing as a perfect Britney, Megan thee Stallion terrifying us as Pinhead from Hellraiser, or several celebs bringing out the Squid Game tracksuits, high levels of creativity and style are on display this year.

See our favorite celeb costumes below:

Hailey Bieber

The model channeled Britney Spears in several of the pop star's most popular video looks.

Chloë

The "Have Mercy" singer dressed as Lola, Angelina Jolie's sultry character from the 2004 animated film Shark Tale, and as Betty Boop.

Kendall Jenner

The model embodied a high-fashion Corpse Bride, as well as a Martian Girl from the Tim Burton flick Mars Attacks!.

Saweetie

The "My Type" rapper's Catwoman costume got a nod of approval from Halle Berry herself.

Harry Styles

Theo Wargo//Getty Images

The rock star dressed up as Dorothy, ruby slippers and all, for his Harryween concert in LA.

Megan thee Stallion

The "Savage" rapper wore both creepy and glamorous looks as Pinhead from Hellraiser and Cruella de Vil.

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker

The newly-engaged couple went as Sex Pistols bassist Sid Vicious and his girlfriend Nancy Spungen.

Cardi B

The "WAP" rapper's Morticia Adams look is made entirely of medal, courtesy of Natalia Fedner Haute Couture.

Lizzo

The "Rumors" singer stepped out in an impressive Baby Yoda costume.

Lisa and Jisoo of BLACKPINK

Lisa looks creepily similar to the Squid Game robot, while Jisoo channelled Lisa's solo debut LALISA.

Chrissy Teigen and John Legend

The couple showed out in their Adams family costumes, featuring their daughter Luna as Wednesday.

Jamie Lee Curtis

Jon Kopaloff//Getty Images

The Halloween actor paid homage to her mom Janet Leigh's role as Marion Crane in the horror classic Psycho, complete with a blood-splattered shower curtain.

Hayley Kiyoko

The singer is a dead ringer for Squid Game character Kang Sae-byeok in a shag wig and 067 tracksuit.

Kerry Washington

The Scandal actress also went the Squid Game route, donning a green tracksuit with Seong Gi-hun's number.

Yara Shahidi

The Grown-ish actor wore Aaliyah's iconic Tommy Hilfiger look.

Kaavia James Union Wade

Gabrielle Union's daughter recreated Adele's stunning Schiaparelli look from basketball player Anthony Davis' wedding.

Ciara

The singer posted her own performances of "Bidi Bidi Bom Bom" by Tejano legend Selena and "No Scrubs" by R&B group TLC.

Doja Cat

Robin L Marshall//Getty Images

The "Kiss Me More" singer dressed up as cartoon divas Miss Bellum from The Powerpuff Girls and Princess Kida from Atlantis: The Lost Empire.

Ariana Grande

The "Positions" singer went Old Hollywood as the monster from the 1954 film Creature from the Black Lagoon.

Sophie Turner, Joe Jonas, and Olivia De Jonge

The trio dressed as Isabella, Paolo, and Lizzie from The Lizzie McGuire Movie.

Lil Nas X

The Montero rapper donned a blonde wig to dress as basketball player Seth Powers from the Nickelodeon show Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide.

Normani

The "Wild Side" singer captioned her sexy goldfish pic, "Nemo’s girl."

Tamron Hall

The talk show host got props from Cardi herself for her costume, duping the rapper's bejeweled pregnancy announcement look from this year's BET Awards.
