What is Ubah Hassan's net worth?

Meet Ubah, a 35-year-old Somalian model, who moved to Seattle in 1994 with the help of a sponsor, but unfortunately, her mother and siblings couldn't join her. They went to Norway instead after her mother got remarried. One day, while she was in a park, a photographer noticed her and saw her potential as a model. That's when Ubah got signed by Click Model Management in New York City. From there, she started doing photoshoots and walking the runways for fashion shows. In May 2008, she gained recognition as Model.com's 'Model of the Week'. Soon after, she became the face of Ralph Lauren's Spring Summer'09 campaign. Ubah has also worked with other famous designers like Oscar de la Renta, Rachel Roy, Malan Breton, Betsey Johnson, and Gucci. Nowadays, she is signed with Richard's International Model Management in Vancouver. It's estimated that Ubah's net worth falls between $700,000 and $1 million.
