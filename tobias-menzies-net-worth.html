Tobias Menzies Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Tobias Menzies net worth:

$12 million

Tobias Menzies is an English actor. His acting career began in the late 1990s. His debut was on the TV series Casualty. After roles on shows and TV films, he landed the part of Marcus Junius Brutus on the TV series Rome. He also had small roles in a couple of feature films like Casino Royale and Atonement. He went on to have guest roles on the TV shows Spooks, The Deep, Any Human Heart, and The Shadow Line. Subsequently he joined the main cast ofEternal Law. In the 2010s, he became more active on TV. He started playing Edmure Tully on Game of Thrones and Frank Randall on Outlander.
