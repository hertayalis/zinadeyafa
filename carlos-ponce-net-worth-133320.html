About

Puerto Rican actor known for his roles in Televisa soap operas. He has appeared in several comedy films, including Rio and Just My Luck. From 2016 to 2017 he played Manuel in American telenovela Silvana sin lana.

Before Fame

He earned his first acting role on the Puerto Rican TV series, Guadalupe.

Trivia

He sang "Belle Notte" in the 2001 sequel, Lady and the Tramp II: Scamp's Adventure.

Family Life

He was married to Veronica Rubio from 1999 to 2010. The couple had four children. From 2010 to 2016, Ponce dated Colombian Actress Ximena Duque.

Associated With

He voice acted alongside Jamie Foxx in the 2011 animated film Rio.
