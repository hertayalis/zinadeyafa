Publish date: 2024-05-22

Paige VanZant has revealed the astronomical amount of money she makes through her OnlyFans account.

The former UFC star and bare-knuckle boxer claims she made more in one day on the content-sharing site than she did during her entire career as a fighter.

“Yes, OnlyFans has definitely been my largest source of income,” VanZant said on the OnlyStans Show.

“I would say combined in my fighting career, I think I’ve made more money in 24 hours on OnlyFans than I have in my entire fighting career.”

The 29-year-old made her UFC debut in November 2014 when she was a 3-1 prospect with the same dream as many budding MMA fighters who want to become a world champion.

Less than a year later, VanZant was a bonified superstar after picking up three consecutive wins and a legion of new fans due to her fighting skills, good looks, and endearing personality.

A sponsorship deal from Reebok quickly came her way and she got the chance to appear on the popular reality show ‘Dancing with the Stars' in 2016.

Unfortunately, VanZant’s rising popularity coincided with a dip in form that saw her lose fights against Rose Namajunas, Michelle Waterson, Jessica-Rose Clark, and Amanda Ribas.

Despite exiting MMA’s premier promotion with an unremarkable 5-4 record in 2020, ’12 Gauge’ was one of the hottest free agents in all of sport.

Ultimately, she decided to sign with Bare-Knuckle Fighting Championship which paid her $400,000 per fight - ten times more than she was earning in the UFC.
