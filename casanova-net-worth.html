Casanova Net Worth: $4 Million

Casanova is an American rapper from Brooklyn, New York. He released his debut studio album Commissary in 2018 and his mixtape Be Safe Tho in 2017. His first original song “Don’t Run” gained attention and he was signed by Jay-Z’s label Roc Nation in 2016. Casanova has collaborated with several artists including Chris Brown, G-Eazy, and Tory Lanez. He has also started his own company 2x Entertainment and has signed Jah Dwella and Lamont Sincere. Despite being involved in gangs and dropping out of school in 8th grade, Casanova has built a successful music career with a net worth of $4 million.

Some of Casanova’s popular singles include “Line Me”, “The Old 50”, “Go Best Friend”, “Left, Right”, “Set Trippin”, and “2Am”.
