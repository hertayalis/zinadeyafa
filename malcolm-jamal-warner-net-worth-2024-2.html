Early Life and Career Beginnings

The Cosby Show and Financial Success

The Cosby Show was a massive hit, running for eight seasons from 1984 to 1992. Warner’s role as Theo Huxtable not only made him a household name but also significantly contributed to his net worth. His earnings from the show are estimated to be in the millions.
