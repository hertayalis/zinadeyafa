Jennifer Lopez Declares There's No Such Thing As Too Many Ruffles in a Jellyfish-esque Gown

Publish date: 2024-07-30

Jennifer Lopez is bringing the fun back to the red carpet.

The movie star arrived at an event in Mexico City to promote her latest film, Netflix action flick Atlas, wearing a playful white ensemble. In particular, the look revolved around a billowing long-sleeved gown made out of organic silk mousseline. Covering the entire length of the dress were tiers of bouncy ruffles.

The piece featured an array of other trendy details, from the tie-front closure to the sheer mesh skirt. Its V neckline also plunged so low, it nearly grazed Lopez’s belly button.

The “Can’t Get Enough” singer’s choice of accessories were just as glamorous as her Chloé dress, with Lopez opting for a pair of diamond drop earrings by Anabela Chan and platform peep-toe heels in a nude shade.

Lopez attended Atlas’s premiere on Monday, where she turned up on the red carpet in an impeccable two-piece from Toronto-based luxury brand Greta Constantine. The set included a strapless ivory top and a sweeping long black skirt with a trumpet silhouette. She kicked it up another notch with plenty of jewelry encrusted with diamonds and massive green gemstones.

Axelle/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Her solo appearance on Monday came amid widespread speculation over the status of her marriage to Ben Affleck, whom she wed in 2022. “They just have very different approaches when it comes to media attention. Ben hates all attention and it makes him very uncomfortable,” a source told People about the issues the couple have been facing in their relationship. “Jennifer has always had a different approach.”

While Affleck has thus far not publicly joined Lopez on the press tour for Atlas, he has been spotted filming The Accountant 2 in Los Angeles.
