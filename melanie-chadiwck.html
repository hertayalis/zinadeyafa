Melanie Rud - Byrdie

Publish date: 2024-06-01

Chicago, Illinois

  • New York University
  • Melanie is a contributing writer for Byrdie where she covers all things skincare—from buzzy ingredients to acne advice.
  • Melanie has over 10 years of experience working in the beauty editorial industry. Over the years, her work has appeared in such digital and print publications as Real Simple, Shape, Allure, and Better Homes & Gardens.
  • She holds a BA in Journalism and English from New York University

Before moving to Chicago, Melanie held staff positions at Shape, Good Housekeeping, and Health magazines. Today, she is a contributing writer for Byrdie where she covers all things skincare, interviewing top dermatologists to get to the bottom of the latest trends and products. You can also find her freelance work in Allure, Pure Wow, Refinery29, Well+Good, Men's Fitness, Domino, Martha Stewart Weddings, Oprah Magazine, Real Simple, and Women's Health.

Melanie holds a BA in Journalism and English from New York University.
