Nia Jax Fires Off Profane Tweet At Shayna Baszler

Publish date: 2024-07-30

Nia Jax and Shayna Baszler didn’t really get along very well when WWE first paired them up. This resulted in a WWE Women’s Tag Team Title run, but the two then split up again. This time Baszler took Nia Jax out of action. Now Jax is responding and she’s not happy.

The Irresistible Force is now out of action for an undetermined period of time. We’ll have to see what happens in this situation, but she hasn’t forgotten about what Shayna Baszler did to her. She fired off a very short tweet directed at the Queen of Spades.

You are a literal piece of shit @QoSBaszler

Baszler seemed a bit conflicted when she destroyed Jax’s arm. It was almost like she couldn’t believe what she did, but now it seems that Baszler will find her own stories apart from Jax. That doesn’t mean Nia Jax is cool with the situation.
